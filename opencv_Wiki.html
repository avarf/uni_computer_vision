Import(Read) Image

  • Import or load image


    img = cv2.imread('photo.jpeg', cv2.IMREAD_GRAYSCALE)
    img = cv2.imread('photo.jpeg', cv2.IMREAD_COLOR)

  • Show image


    cv2.imshow('image', img)

  • Save Image



Working With Video

  • Access to video cam


    cap = cv2.VideoCapture(0)
    ret, frame = cap.read()
    cap.release()

  • Save Video

  • Grayscale conversion


    gray = cv2.cvtColor(frame, cv2.COLOR_BGR2GRAY)

  • Replace one part of picture:


    subimg = gray[50:430, 50:590]
    frame[50:430, 50:590] = subimg
